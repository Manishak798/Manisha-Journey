About Me


Passionate MCA Student | Problem-Solver | Full Stack Developer


Greetings! ✨ ✨


I am Manisha, a final year MCA student at CIITM Jaipur, eager to make a meaningful impact in the world of technology. As a BCA pass-out with a strong foundation in problem-solving and a focus on honing my skills in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and Full Stack Development, I am poised to contribute to innovative projects and excel in the industry.


Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a deep passion for problem-solving and have dedicated my time to mastering DSA concepts with C++ as my preferred programming language. I am constantly seeking opportunities to apply my skills and expand my knowledge in creating efficient algorithms and data structures that optimize performance. Moreover, my enthusiasm for Full Stack Development has allowed me to gain practical experience in building dynamic and user-friendly web applications.


I am well-versed in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as back-end frameworks like Node.js and Express.js. I am constantly exploring new technologies and frameworks to stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends.


Apart from technical expertise, I possess strong leadership qualities that have been nurtured through various team projects and group activities. I believe in fostering collaboration and driving team synergy to achieve common goals. As a proactive learner, I am always open to new challenges and thrive in an environment that encourages continuous growth and learning.


If you are looking for a motivated and dedicated individual with a strong foundation in DSA, Full Stack Development, and problem-solving skills, I would love to connect with you. Let's explore how we can contribute to the world of technology together and make a lasting impact. Feel free to reach out to me through LinkedIn or in the contact me section.


Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
